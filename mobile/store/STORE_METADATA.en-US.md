Track every studio session with type, duration, mood, and notes. Build momentum with streaks, review your weekly progress, and follow clear next actions so your work compounds over time.

- Maintain streaks and weekly consistency goals

Prodify is built for real producer workflows: simple enough to use daily, powerful enough to keep you shipping.
