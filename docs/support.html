Subscriptions

Prodify requires an active subscription. To manage or cancel your plan, open Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone. You can also use Restore Purchases on the Prodify paywall.

Delete your account

In the app: Profile > Settings & legal > Delete account. Deletion is permanent and removes your sessions, streaks, and friendships.
